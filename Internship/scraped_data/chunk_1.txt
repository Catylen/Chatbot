Capitol Technology University is the only independent university in Maryland dedicated to engineering, computer science, information technology and management of technology. Founded in 1927, Capitol Technology University is a regionally accredited institution offering associate, bachelor's, master's and doctorate degrees, as well as professional development training and certificates. The university's 52-acre campus is located in Laurel, Maryland, a suburban setting midway between Washington, DC and Baltimore. Full-time undergraduate on-campus students are eligible for a tuition guarantee (tuition will not go up more than 1% per year) and a job guarantee.
